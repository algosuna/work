Our guarantee is simple: Purchase a dog from us at full price, and if at any time during the first two years you are not satisfied simply return the dog to us, in good condition, and we will replace it with another puppy.

Obviously, this guarantee is void if the dog has been abused, neglected or injured. Pups must be paid for in full by 6 weeks of age or you will lose your deposit, and the pup will be resold. We Ship All our puppies and dogs. Picking your puppy will be made at 7 weeks and puppies will be allowed to go to their new homes at 8 weeks. Puppies will have been wormed and given their first inoculation.

We have our puppies checked by a Vet 3 times before they leave us (our Babies are checked when they get their health cert.). Our vet checks the following on every puppy: 
  • Eyes: Rolling lids, entropian
  • Mouth: Teeth, glands, tonsils
  • Ears: Clear
  • Nose: Stenosias
  • Stomach: Umbilical & inguinal hernias, palpate
  • Leg joints: Dysplasia, luxation
  • Heart: Murmur, normal rhythm
  • Lungs: Normal sounds
  • Testicles (male): Dropped
